How predictions work

Revenue: 7-day MA + linear regression → project 7 days.
Growth: exponential smoothing (α=0.3).
Churn: recency×0.4 + frequency×0.35 + monetary×0.25.
Reorder Point: avg daily sales × lead time + safety stock.
Days of Stock: current stock / avg daily demand.
Stock Turnover: total sold / avg inventory.
